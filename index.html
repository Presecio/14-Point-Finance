Business Finance

We specialise exclusively in commercial and business finance. No personal loans. No mortgages. No insurance. Just smarter business funding — structured, efficient, and tailored to your growth objectives.

As an independent finance broker, 14 Point Finance works for you — not the banks. We access a panel of over 50 lenders including major banks, non-bank lenders, and specialist funders to find terms that suit your cash flow, asset profile, and growth plans.
